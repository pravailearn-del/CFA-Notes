Real Estate and Infrastructure

Module Overview: This module covers real estate and infrastructure as alternative asset classes. Understand their features, investment forms (direct ownership, REITs, funds), valuation approaches, income characteristics, and role in diversified portfolios.

Explain features and characteristics of real estate
Explore real estate asset types (residential, commercial, industrial, retail) and key features including heterogeneity, high unit value, management intensity, illiquidity, and location dependency. Understand different investment forms: direct ownership, REITs (equity and mortgage), private real estate funds, and real estate operating companies.
